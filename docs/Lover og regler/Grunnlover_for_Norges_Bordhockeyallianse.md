FOUNDING CONSTITUTION OF NORGES BORDHOCKEYALLIANSE (NBA)

§1. The organisation’s name is Norges Bordhockeyallianse, abbreviated
NBA. NBA was founded on the 1st May, 1996 and is a continuation of the
defunct Norges Bordhockeyforbund, which folded on the same day.

§2. NBA’s aim is to unite the Norwegian table hockey community and
promote the continued growth of the sport in Norway and internationally.
NBA endorses exclusively tournaments played with the Stiga Play Off game
and define “table hockey” as the sport practised on these boards only.

§3. NBA’s headquarters are at all times the home address of the current
Chairman.

§4. The Board and Chairman are elected through simple majority by a
single vote of the annual National Conference.

§5. The National Conference is held during the weekend of the Norwegian
Championships. Anyone who is present at the Norwegian Championships and
represented on the national ranking list has one vote.

§6. The Board is principally composed by the following delegates:

- Chairman
- International delegate
- Maintenance delegate
- Economic delegate
- Public Relations delegate
- Secretary

The National Conference may opt to expand the Board to include other
delegates.

§7. The NBA can be dissolved through a ¾ majority vote by the National
Conference.

Amendment as of 7th June, 2005:

§8. The NBA acknowledges the International Table Hockey Federation
(ITHF) as the only international federation governing the sport on an
international scale. NBA acknowledges the World Cup team and individual
tournament sponsored, endorsed or arranged by the ITHF.
